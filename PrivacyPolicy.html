Privacy Policy (Software Projects Android App)

Software Projects Android App will not get any of your personal information from your phone e.g.; your contact detail, contacts or location etc.

This app will have access to only that information which you will input to it directly.

The information input by you on this app will be stored on a server in order to back up the information and to use during communication of project.

An email regarding email address confirmation will be sent on the email address which you will input to this app.

During the reset password process an email will also be sent for confirmation on the email address you will input.
